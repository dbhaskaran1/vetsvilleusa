About us

We believe in our veterans. They are capable of great things.

Our mission and purpose is to bridge the gap to guide them to a new destiny.

Veterans face a truly challenging transition when they return to civilian life. They must reinvent themselves. They go from the military's structured routine to a completely unstructured environment where they are under constant pressure to integrate into independence and self-sufficiency. Veterans must make life-changing decisions quickly, many times without coaching and guidance that hold the key to options that lead to successful outcomes. VetsvilleUSA wants to eliminate uncertainty and doubts in their minds. We want vets armed with confidence and self-worth ready to compete in today's job market, making wise decisions that pay dividends which change their lives.

VetsvilleUSA provides the required services and resources. We provide life coaching and counseling to assess goals. We provide housing in our veterans living centers, while receiving quality college coursework or vocational training either online or in classrooms. This helps vets advance towards employment or entrepreneurship, receiving a secure a place in life's journey toward financial security.

VetsvilleUSA wants optimum opportunity for each of our veterans. They deserve OUR best as they gave us their best. Our veterans wear their "CAP" proudly. "CAP" is Career Action Plan, a step-by-step approach to their career success and financial security. We counsel vets in developing goal-oriented options which bring into focus training through our educational partnerships in one of many areas including: Culinary Arts, Business Management, Hospitality Operations, Hydroponics and Horticulture.

Our entrepreneurship program develops offers opportunities for vets to run their Freight Farms (the latest and greatest hydroponics program in America!), revitalizing under-performing hotels across the country (utilizing skills learnt in training covering all facets of hotel operations and restaurant services management). We thus transform the vets into business leaders and help them realize their goals, with VetsvilleUSA being their "bridge" to a solid, secure future.

  • Aim - Improve quality of life of our Veterans
  • How? - Life coaching/counseling +vocational training/college programs = employment
  • Target Audience - Calling out to all veterans!

VetsvilleUSA Living and Resource Center recruits men and women who are transitioning from active duty to civilian life when they are making critical civilian career decisions. More and more of these potential students are recognizing that they need to upgrade their education to compete in our tough job market. This is the ideal time for you to connect with mission-oriented students who are seeking a military-friendly school that accepts their military benefits.

Our Veterans Living and Resort Centers provide housing solutions for veterans through our operation of formerly underperforming hotels. We want to build a bridge for your institution to be their choice as they are poised to begin training for their civilian career. VetsvilleUSA can connect you directly with a continuous stream of qualified veterans who are actively seeking the best way to leverage their benefits for the educational opportunity that is right for them.
