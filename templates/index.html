About us

We believe in our veterans. We believe they are capable of great things.

Vetsville USA Decatur focuses on assisting our Veterans transition from the military to civilian life through vocational training. We also offer substance abuse counseling where necessary. Veterans feel comfortable at our training center because they are surrounded by Veterans that they can relate to. We are a support center that will assist you back to civilian life. In addition, we will follow up after your training and counseling to make sure you are adjusting to a new life. You are always WELCOME at Vetsville USA .

  • Aim - Improve quality of life of our Veterans
  • How? - Vocational training, counseling and placements
  • Target Audience - Calling out to all veterans!
