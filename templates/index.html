About us

Some words about us

We believe in our veterans. We believe they are capable of great things. They have proven this time and again, in war after war. It is now our turn to help vets successfully make the transition into civilian life and help them support themselves.

VETSVILLE USA DECATUR focuses on assisting our Veterans transition from the military to civilian life through vocational training and substance abuse counseling. Veterans feel comfortable at our training center because they are surrounded by Veterans that they can relate to. We are a support center that will assist you back to civilian life. In addition, we will follow up after your training and counseling to make sure you are adjusting to a new life. You are always WELCOME at VETSVILLE USA DECATUR.

  • Mission - Improve quality of life of our Veterans
  • Skills - Vocational training, counseling and placements
  • Clients - Satisfied clients thanks to our experience
